ANNUAL FINANCIAL REPORT
FISCAL YEAR 2022
ACME CORPORATION

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

ACME Corporation is pleased to present its Annual Financial Report for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2022. Despite global economic challenges, our company delivered solid financial results, demonstrating the strength of our business model and the success of our strategic initiatives.

Total revenue for fiscal year 2022 was $1,162 million, representing a 5.6% increase compared to $1,100 million in fiscal year 2021. This growth was primarily driven by our core product lines and expansion in key markets.

Net income was $167.4 million, up 4.6% from $160 million in the previous year. This improvement reflects our continued focus on operational efficiency, resulting in a profit margin of 14.4% compared to 14.5% in fiscal year 2021.

INCOME STATEMENT

Revenue and Sales
Total revenue was $1,162 million for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2022, compared to $1,100 million for fiscal year 2021, representing an increase of $62 million or 5.6%.

Revenue by segment:
- Product Sales: $813.4 million (70% of total revenue)
- Services: $290.5 million (25% of total revenue)
- Licensing: $58.1 million (5% of total revenue)

Cost of Revenue
Cost of revenue was $710 million for fiscal year 2022, compared to $671 million for fiscal year 2021. As a percentage of total revenue, cost of revenue remained stable at 61% in both fiscal years.

Gross Profit
Gross profit was $452 million for fiscal year 2022, compared to $429 million for fiscal year 2021. Gross margin remained consistent at 39% in both fiscal years.

Operating Expenses
Total operating expenses were $232 million for fiscal year 2022, compared to $220 million for fiscal year 2021.

Operating expenses by category:
- Research and Development: $116.2 million (10% of total revenue)
- Sales and Marketing: $81.3 million (7% of total revenue)
- General and Administrative: $34.5 million (3% of total revenue)

Operating Income
Operating income was $220 million for fiscal year 2022, compared to $209 million for fiscal year 2021, representing an increase of $11 million or 5.3%.

Net Income
Net income was $167.4 million for fiscal year 2022, compared to $160 million for fiscal year 2021, representing an increase of $7.4 million or 4.6%. Earnings per share increased to $3.35 from $3.20 in the previous year.

Key equity components:

CASH FLOW STATEMENT

Cash Flow from Operating Activities
Net cash provided by operating activities was $290 million for fiscal year 2022, compared to $275 million for fiscal year 2021.

Cash Flow from Financing Activities

CONCLUSION AND OUTLOOK

ACME Corporation delivered solid financial performance in fiscal year 2022, with growth in revenue, improved profitability, and strong cash flow generation. Looking ahead to fiscal year 2023, we expect continued growth driven by our innovation pipeline and expansion into new markets. We anticipate revenue growth of 6-8% and a further improvement in profit margins.

The company remains committed to creating long-term shareholder value through strategic investments, operational excellence, and disciplined capital allocation. We are confident in our ability to navigate market challenges and capitalize on emerging opportunities in the coming year.
